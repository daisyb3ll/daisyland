The Big O!; Data Structures Simulator

Intro to Data Structures Simulator! Earn the degree- or else!!

Data Structures Simulator, is a project my best friend/ roommate/ peer/ classmate/ teammate Sarah Yoon and I worked on as a part of our submission for TreeHacks. As game enthusiasts (Sarah- game development minor, Daisy- video game enjoyer), we were very excited about our project- which we created as a part of the education track of the event. In our game- you get to be a part of our school's data structure class, and embark on your own journey to learning data structures runtimes. Taught by our very own professor,Rene (hello prof.rene if you see this please don't fail me please).

Created for Stanford's Treehacks'23.
