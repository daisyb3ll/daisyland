about daisy

My name is Daisy. I’m a first-gen So-Cal based artist. I’m tech-curious and a habitual creative—I doodle to code and code when I'm doodling—and I’m always looking to learn more means of expression towards creative solutions. I do UX/UI research and design, I also draw, prototype, program, and sew. Not always in that order.

How I Got Here

August 2021

First Gen in STEM

Started my CS journey at Chapman University! (paws up) I got my first laptop ever and started taking Computer Science classes for the first time ever! Thank you for your patience, Prof. Studebaker.

Jan 2022

Daisy The Game Developer

After learning some basic languages and taking a Unity class, I added a Game Development minor and found my niche making assets and animated game sprites.

October 2022

Daisy The Project Manager

I realized I'm not half bad at managing teams and worked with my peers to get passion projects going and get more involved in the community!

October 2023

Daisy The Software Engineer

I started developing my own programming projects and experimenting with different languages and frameworks in order to broaden my skillset!

January 2024

Daisy The Designer

After broadening my skillset, I realized I could add more of a creative pop to things. I reflected on what made me happy as both a programmer and designer. I've since worked on finding my niche and have created my own space in the World Wide Web. I've found my niche as someone who aspires to research & learn, design, experiment, develop, and include. I am a creative technologist. Welcome to my work.

2025

Daisy The Creative Technologist

I’m a chronic doodler. On my notes, homework, papers, and even pants. Always the creative, I surprised everyone with a degree in Software Engineering (myself included). I thought I was stuck in tech world forever, but quickly realized I’d never left my roots in art—or that there was never a border between the two. I’ve since done work in game dev art, UX/UI design and development, 3D modeling, hardware prototyping, fabrication, and more.
