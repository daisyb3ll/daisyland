about daisy

Hi my name is Daisy.
I am a first-generation student at Chapman University where I major in Software Engineering and minor in Peace Studies.

In my free time, I’m a freelance graphic designer & illustrator and work as a UX/UI student researcher. I'm a self-proclaimed creative technologist and aspiring designer, researcher, and front-end developer. I like to experiment with web design, pixel art, airbrushing, novice 3D modeling, and sculpting.

Away from tech, I like skating, reading, rock music, gaming, and axolotls. I;m currently obsessed with One Piece, Pierce Brown's Light Bringer, and Nacho Libre. Im on a life-long mission to discover the comfiest desk chair, so if you have any reccomendations please reach out!
